Delicious Grilled Hamburger

A simple burger for simple tastes.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon Worcesterchire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon liquid smoke flavoring
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt to taste

Recipe

  1. Preheat a grill for high heat.
  2. In a medium bowl, lightly mix together the ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, liquid smoke and garlic powder.
  3. Form into 3 patties, handling the meat minimally.
  4. Brush both sides of each patty with some oil, and season with seasoned salt.
  5. Place the patties on the grill grate, and cook for about 5 minutes per side, until well done.
